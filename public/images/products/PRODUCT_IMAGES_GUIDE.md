## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800 pixels (square) or 800x600 pixels (landscape)
- **File Size**: Keep under 200KB for optimal loading
- **Background**: White or transparent preferred
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional product photography

## How to Add Images

1. Get your product images (photos or download from manufacturer websites)
2. Rename them according to the exact filenames listed above
3. Save them in: `c:\Users\nihal\Desktop\intern 3\analogics-website\public\images\products\`
4. The website will automatically display them!

## Tips for Finding Images

- Search for product names on manufacturer websites
- Use stock photo sites like Unsplash, Pexels (free)
- Take photos of actual products if available
- Ensure you have rights to use the images
